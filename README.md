# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project

This HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of employee attrition, workforce demographics, compensation trends, and retention risk factors. The dashboard helps HR teams and business leaders identify key drivers of employee turnover and make data-driven workforce decisions.

**🔹 Key Features:**
• Executive Attrition Overview
• Workforce & Compensation Analysis
• Employee Risk & Retention Insights
• Attrition by Department, Job Role & Gender
• Career Growth vs Attrition Analysis
• Work-Life Balance Impact
• Environment Satisfaction Analysis
• Business Travel & Overtime Impact
• Interactive Filters and Drill-Down Capabilities

**📈 Key Insights:**
✔ Overtime and frequent business travel are major contributors to attrition.
✔ Employees with limited career growth opportunities show higher turnover risk.
✔ Environment satisfaction and work-life balance significantly impact employee retention.
✔ Department-level analysis helps identify high-risk workforce segments.

**🛠 Tools Used:**
• Power BI
• Power Query
• DAX
• Data Modeling
